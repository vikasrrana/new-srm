What is TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxSRMNCR, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxSRMNCR event, TED Talks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.

The Road Not Taken

If you’re standing at a Y fork in a path, and the path is well-trodden in one direction but overgrown in the other - which one would you choose? Your heart lub-dubs, the clock tick-tocks and you fear that your one wrong decision, that very second, might land you in a whirlpool of regret. So you go with the well trodden one. Because that is the way majority of us take decisions.However, the survivors or successful people as we call them, look beyond their fears. It is not that they do different things, but they do the same things differently. It requires a little more courage, a little more self belief to pull through. People who had faith in their own selves, who irrespective of all the odds, took the road less travelled because they knew that ‘out-of-the-way’ is their ‘only way’. They took “The Road Not Taken” and that has manifestly brought about all the difference: the difference that is bliss for them. We adore the spirit, courage and determination of such people. Let’s embrace the strength and the will of these people who fearlessly take the decision that’d bring all the difference and make their ordinary life not so ordinary anymore in ways they can't imagine. We present to you, TEDxSRMNCR precisely focusing on the much deliberated topic: "The Road Not Taken".
